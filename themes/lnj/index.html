ABOUT LNJ DENIM
From Fibre to Finishing

LNJ Denim’s manufacturing facility was established in 2007 as a part of RSWM Limited, the flagship company of LNJ Bhilwara Group, is one of the largest textile manufacturers in India and exports to over 78 countries around the world. Its 10 manufacturing plants, with 505,000 spindles and 176 looms, produce high-quality cotton, mélange, synthetic and novelty yarns, along with suiting and denim fabrics.
